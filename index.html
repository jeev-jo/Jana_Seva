About Us

Bridging the Digital Divide

Bringing government services closer to every citizen, one application at a time.

E Sevana Kendram Korome began with a simple mission: no citizen should have to travel hours or stand in endless queues to access the government services they deserve. Located in the heart of Korome village, we serve as the officially authorized Common Service Centre for the entire region, trusted by thousands of families who depend on us for everything from Aadhaar enrollment to pension applications.

Our centre is staffed by trained Digital Seva operators who understand both the technology and the people. We process over fifty different government services with care, accuracy, and speed. Whether you need a fresh passport, a corrected PAN card, a caste certificate, or help enrolling in a PM welfare scheme, we handle the entire process from start to finish so you don't have to navigate confusing government portals alone.

In a region where digital literacy is still growing, we believe the government's digital services should reach every doorstep. That's why we've invested in the latest biometric equipment, high-speed connectivity, and a welcoming space where every visitor is treated with respect. From elderly citizens applying for pensions to young students downloading their mark sheets, we serve them all with the same dedication.

Since our inception, we have served over five thousand citizens, processing applications with a success rate that speaks for itself. Our transparent pricing, quick turnaround, and personal attention have earned us a reputation as the most trusted service centre in the area. When you visit Jana Seva, you're not just visiting a service centre — you're visiting a neighbor who cares about getting things done right.
